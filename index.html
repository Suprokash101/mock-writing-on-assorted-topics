1. Title(It's all about tickling their buying bones):

Thats correct. You heard it right! You entice your readers with a crisp and no nonsense "Title". If that's done, there you have accomplished the half of it. How about lengths??? The expert opinion is "65 character". You say whatever you wanna say, but finish it within 65 characters.

2. Subheadings (Those unsung hero's):

Subheadings are the hidden cornerstones of your precious article...You categorize your content( at least you try )....Then provide them with appropriate subheadings...

3. How long that can be?:

Well, let me be frank with you, Google loves large articles (Ya, there I said it!)....If you ask me, I won't go too far...Why? Cause, I don't want my readers to be beaten to death with long boring gory details. You better contain your content within 3000 words...Don't be too short (e.g. less then 300 words). It won't hurt if it is 700-800 words long, to be on the safe side.

5. Keywords (Look ma! I found my keys.):

Say "Hi" to "Keywords". The key to successful content writing is nothing but choosing the "appropriate" keywords. Of course, It must shed light on your content's main theme. It has to be 1-2.5% of your entire text (Experts call it "Keyword Density"). Once you have figured out your keywords, then play with it...juggle your thoughts around 'em....Try to use 'em in the title, headers, sub-headers, main texts.... You will find your way, I have no doubt about you!...You can try these tools for improving your skills on that:

  • Keyword Density Checker
  • On Page SEO optimization

6. Optimize your content images (It's all in a days work):
